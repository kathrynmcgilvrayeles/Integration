Drafting Documents and Emails

How it's used: Copilot drafts initial versions of emails, reports, and presentations based on your prompts. It can also refine tone or shorten text.

Benefits for work tasks: This significantly boosts your efficiency by automating initial drafting, allowing you to dedicate more time to strategic thinking and core professional responsibilities.

Summarising Information and Meetings

How it's used: Copilot provides real-time meeting summaries, highlights key points, and identifies action items from recordings in Teams. It can also summarise long email threads from meeting recordings.

Benefits for work tasks: Meeting notes can be produced more quickly, thus improving communication to the team.

Brainstorming and Idea Generation

How it's used: It acts as a thought partner, generating ideas for campaigns, product features, or problem-solving approaches when prompted. Use your existing Microsoft docs and make improvements with Copilot, making suggestions as you make changes.

Benefits for work tasks: This fosters innovation, helps you overcome creative blocks, and accelerates the initial stages of your creative projects.

Data Analysis and Insights

How it's used: It assists with data analysis in Excel or Sheets, identifying trends, suggesting formulas, and recommending charts for better understanding. It can suggest or write formulas to improve the analysis and output of data and tables.

Benefits for work tasks: This empowers you to gain quicker, data-driven insights, even if you don't have advanced analytical skills or strong skills using formulas.
